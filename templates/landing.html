Overview

Why MewDrive exists

We built MewDrive to remove friction from everyday file movement in classrooms, labs, and local teams. Instead of hunting for pendrives or juggling multiple cloud accounts, you get a single, controlled vault in your LAN.

Centralized
Keep documents organized in one familiar place.
Seamless sharing
